Festivals of Karnataka


Ganesha Chaturthi

When is it celebrated: August or September
Location: All across Karnataka and other parts of India

Mysore Dasara

When is it celebrated: 10th day of the month of Ashvin.
Location:Mysore, Karnataka.

Ugadi

When is it celebrated: March or April
Location: Karnataka, Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

Hampi Festival

When is it celebrated: January.
Location: Hampi, Karnataka

Sri Vithappa Fair

When is it celebrated:The fourteen and fifteen day of the Ashwija month.
Location:Vithappa Village, Karnataka.

Makar Sankranti

When is it celebrated: 14th or 15th of January.
Location: All across Karnataka.

Vairamundi Festival

When is it celebrated:March-April.
Location: Sri Cheluvanarayana Swamy Temple, Melkote Town, Karnataka.

Kambala Festival

When is it celebrated November to March
Location:Kasargod in Kerala, Udupi in Karnataka, and the coastal districts of Dakshina Kannada.

Karaga Festival

When is it celebrated: March or April
Location Varthur, Narasapura, Bangarpet, Ramagondanahalli and other parts of the state.
