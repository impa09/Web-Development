Atthur Church

 St. Lawrence Shrine Basilica or Attur Basilica (Portuguese: São Lourenço de Carcoal, English: St. Lawrence of Karkala) is a Roman Catholic church in Karkala, India. It was built in 1759 and is purported to be the site of many miracles. It is by characterized its Attur Jatre or Attur Festival.

How to reach Atthur Church?

  St. Lawrence Church Attur is located in Karkala which is just 38 km from Udupi and 54 km from Mangalore. The closest airport to this church would be Mangalore which is reasonably well connected domestically as well as internationally.

Duration: 1-2 hours

Best Season: Atthur jatre or Atthur Festival

Region: Karnataka

Kaup Lighthouse and Beach

  Kapu lighthouse was built in 1901. Kapu lighthouse is 27 meters tall. Constructed on a rock, the lighthouse has stood the test of time.Kapu light house is open to visitors from 4 PM to 6 PM daily. Visitors may go up the stairs and get a bird’s eye view of Kąpu beach.

How to Kaup Lighthouse?

 Kapu is 400 kms from Bengaluru and 45 kms from Mangaluru (nearest airport). Udupi is the nearest railway station (15 kms). Regular buses are available from Mangaluru and Udupi to reach Kapu.

    Duration:1-2 hours

    Best Season: Closed during rainy season

    Region: Karnataka

    Sunset at Malpe Beach

     One of the best things to do at Malpe beach is to take a short, 2-hour trip to the nearby St. Mary’s Islands. A set of 4 small islands, this Malpe beach island is unique because of its exclusive rock formations that cannot be seen anywhere else but the Giant Causeway in Northern Ireland and some places in Iceland.

    How to reach Malpe Beach?

     On the top of the list of exquisite unexplored beaches in India is the Malpe beach. It is located at a distance of 66 kilometres from Mangalore and 6 kilometres from Udupi in Karnataka.

      Duration: 2-3 hours

      Best Season: Closed during rainy season

      Region: Karnataka

      Maravanthe Beach

       Maravanthe wears the look of a fairyland with the picturesque Kodachadri Hills forming a backdrop to the Sauparnika River on one side and miles of unspoilt white sand on the other.

      How to reach Maravanthe Beach?

       Maravanthe is about 420 kms from state capital Bengaluru and about 105 kms from Mangaluru (Nearest airport). Kundapura railway station (20 kms) is the nearest railway connection to Maravanthe.

        Duration:1-2 hours

        Best Season: June – February (Monsoon & Winter)

        Region: Karnataka

        St.Aloysious Chapel

         St Aloysius College was built in 1882. The southern wing of the building formed the Chapel as a place of prayer for students. The Chapel built in 1885 by Rev Father Joseph Willy, is a replica of world renowned Sistine Chapel at Rome.

        How to St.Aloysious Chapel?

         Located right in the middle of the city in all its glory, it was built in 1885, by Reverend Father Joseph Willy. Its location is at the top of the Light House Hill on the campus of St. Aloysius College. The location allows you to get a picturesque view of the Arabian Sea.

          Duration: 1-2 hours

          Best Season: January – December (Any season)

          Region: Karnataka
